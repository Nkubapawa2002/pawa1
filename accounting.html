Tax Computation

🧾 Value Added Tax (VAT) — 18%

VAT returns filed monthly with TRA. Output tax must be remitted by the 20th of the following month.

👤 PAYE & SDL (Salary Tax)

PAYE remitted to TRA by the 7th of the following month. Skills & Development Levy (SDL) is 4% of gross payroll.

🏢 Corporate Income Tax (CIT) — 30%

CIT is an annual tax. This is an estimate based on the selected period. Final CIT return due 6 months after year-end. Quarterly installments apply when annual estimated tax exceeds TZS 3,000,000.

✂ Withholding Tax (WHT)

WHT remitted to TRA within 7 days of payment (resident payee) or 30 days (non-resident).
