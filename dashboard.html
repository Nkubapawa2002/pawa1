    Add a Bus

    Register a new bus under your company. You can add routes to it after saving.

    How it works

    1. Add your bus — fill the form and click Save Bus.

    2. Add routes — once saved, click "Add Route" on the bus card to define where it travels and at what time. Each route automatically creates a return leg.

    3. Add agents — go to the Agents tab to add terminal staff who handle cargo and bookings.

    4. Set API keys — in Settings, paste your Anthropic & VAPI keys so the AI voice agent can take bookings automatically.

    Your Buses & Routes

    Click "Add Route" on a bus card to define its schedule, or "Edit Seats" to design the seat layout on a drag-and-drop canvas.

    Request Trip Cancellation

    Submit a cancellation for a scheduled trip. Your company admin must approve it before any booking is affected.

    You do not have trip cancellation authority. Ask your company admin to enable it for your account in the Team tab.

    Security

    Two-person rule
    Only team members with cancellation authority (set by admin in Team tab) can submit a request. A company admin must then approve it.

    What gets cancelled
    All active bookings for the chosen bus, date, and route are cancelled at once.

    Passenger notification
    After approval, n8n automatically sends SMS cancellation notices to every affected passenger.
