Frequently asked

How fast does the AI agent book me a seat?

Under three minutes from "hello" to ticket SMS, including USSD payment.

Which payment methods are supported?

Vodacom M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa (Mixx by Yas), Airtel Money, Halopesa, AzamPesa, NMB, CRDB, NBC, plus Visa/Mastercard. Cash at the terminal also works.

What if I miss the bus?

You'll receive an automatic call 30 minutes before departure. If you cancel before boarding, choose a free reschedule on the next trip OR an 80% refund.

Is my parcel insured?

Yes. Every parcel is automatically insured. If lost, you recover up to 80% of the declared value.

How does Meet & Locate work?

Open meet.html, create a 6-character room and share it. Both phones drop pins on a Tanzania map and see each other move in real time, with weather, distance, and one-tap navigation.
