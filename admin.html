What do these mean? (Approve · Record month · Enrol on plan · Set fee)

New agents are live the moment they register, for 7 days. Within those 7 days you must Approve them, or their listings auto-hide until you do. Approval is one-time; billing (below) is the ongoing monthly part.

  • Approve — confirm a new agent so they stay live permanently (button turns to "Approved"; click again to revoke). Unapproved agents show "Preview · Nd to approve" and then "Unapproved — hidden" after day 7. The Awaiting approval stat + the Pending approval filter show who still needs you.
  • Set fee — set how much this agent pays per month (TZS). Defaults to the standard fee; use it to charge an agent a custom amount. Does not mark them paid.
  • Enrol on plan — put the agent on the monthly plan: sets their fee and records their billing start. Does not take money or mark them paid.
  • Record month (per-row +1 month) — the one that means "they paid": marks paid, re-activates the account, and pushes Paid until forward by one full month. Each agent is billed on their own timeline — they don't all pay on the same day. Paying early stacks (current expiry + 1 month, no lost days); paying after lapsing starts a fresh month from today.
  • Activate / Deactivate — admin override, independent of payment. Deactivating hides the agent's listings and shows them the reason you type.

Columns

  • Billing start — the day they were first billed / went live (informational; the cycle rolls from each payment, not this date).
  • Status & approval — the live lifecycle badge (Preview · Nd, Unapproved — hidden, Active · Nd left, Expired, Deactivated…) plus the Approve / +1 month / Activate buttons.
  • Billing — status (free / trial / paid / overdue / cancelled). Plan — a free-text tier label. Amount — the monthly fee.
  • Paid until — the exact coverage expiry for this agent. Next due — the date "+1 month" would extend them to. Subscription — a live badge (Active · Nd left, Grace · Nh to pay, Expired, Deactivated…).

Each agent runs on an independent monthly cycle measured from their own payment — no shared due date. Agents can now also pay by mobile money themselves, which auto-does the “Record month” step (identical maths). This panel is for cash/offline payments and overrides. Tick rows (or none = all shown) for a bulk action.
